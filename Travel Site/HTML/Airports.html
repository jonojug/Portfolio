Airports in Chicago

O'Hare International Airport

O'Hare International Airport is one of Chicago's main airport. If you are in the city it is usually within 30-40 minutes car ride. You should get to the airport 1 to 2 hours before your flight to make sure you don't miss it! This is also Chicago's largest airports and used to be the busiest in the world.

Midway International Airport

This is Chicago's second largest Airport, it is smaller only with 5 runways and has a much slower pace compared to ORD. Being smaller, it isn't as up to date as ORD and definitely lame-er.Although it is technically an international airport, it only serves to Mexico and Toronto. So if you want to actually fly somewhere go to O'Hare, but if your lame and flying domestic, Midway would be good for you
