Fun Places in Chicago

Navy Pier

Navy Pier is one of Chicago's most popular attractions. It is 3000 feet long and 292 feet wide for the geeks that love measuring things. Some recognizable features are it's huge ferris wheel, the food stands, and the lake around it.

Shedd Aquarium

Chicago's aquatic zoo is called the Shedd Aquarium, it houses whales, dolphins, and oddly live chicken hatching shows. If you are interested in cool animals and scary fish the Shedd is the best place for you to spend your time. If you like fossils and cool engineering, go to the field museum and if you like planet round object things go to the planetarium.
